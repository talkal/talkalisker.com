# 03. /technical_findings
Below are the critical technical issues flagged during our deep-crawl of the architecture.

- **Finding:** Slow LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) | **Severity:** High
Images on the homepage are not properly compressed or localized, causing a 4.2s delay on 3G connections. Google penalizes any LCP over 2.5s.
**Recommendation:** Implement Next-Gen image formats (WebP) and a modern CDN.

- **Finding:** Missing Alt Text on Case Studies | **Severity:** Low
Several portfolio images lack alt text, missing easy keyword density opportunities and failing basic accessibility screens.
**Recommendation:** Update CMS to require alt-tags before publishing.
