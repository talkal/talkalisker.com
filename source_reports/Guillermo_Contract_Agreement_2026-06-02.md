# 03. /project_roadmap

This schedule dictates the delivery of project milestones.

**Phase 1: Brand Foundation & Strategic Naming (June 1–7)**
Delivery of naming candidates, target audience definition, and brand voice. Includes presentation of two distinct brand/naming directions for strategic choice.

**Phase 2: Full Brand Kit & Visual Identity (June 8–21)**
Delivery of the complete branding kit for the chosen direction, including logo suite, color palette, typography system, visual language, and comprehensive brand guidelines. 

**Phase 3: Web Development & Brand Applications (June 22–28)**
Delivery of the functional coded website (HTML5/CSS3/JS), SEO report, complete UI/UX design system, and brand applications (business cards, LinkedIn banners, email signatures). Custom domain configured on GitHub Pages.

**Phase 4: Final Tweaks & Handover (June 29–July 5)**
Completion of feedback cycles. Final technical handover of all assets, source code, and GitHub repository access. Guillermo assumes 100% intellectual property ownership.
