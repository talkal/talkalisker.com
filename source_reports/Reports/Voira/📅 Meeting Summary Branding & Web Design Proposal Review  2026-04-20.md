## 🎯 Executive Overview

Guillermo met with Tal and Yaeli to review their branding and web design proposal for his new AI digital transformation consulting firm in Madrid. Guillermo expressed interest in their creative approach but pushed back heavily on the €4,500 price point, noting it is significantly above standard Spanish market rates. The ultimate outcome is an agreement that Tal and Yaeli will rescope the project into manageable phases, prioritize a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) deliverable, and return with a newly itemized proposal to better align with Guillermo's budget and operational timeline.
